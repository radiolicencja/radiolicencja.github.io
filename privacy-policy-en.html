How we handle your data

Radiolicencja is a learning app for radio licence exams. We designed it to run fully on your device without collecting personal data.

What we store

  • No account or login is required. We do not collect names, email addresses, or other identifiers.
  • Your learning progress (correct/incorrect answers, last seen timestamps, mastery) is saved locally on your device using on-device storage.
  • No analytics SDKs or advertising networks are integrated.

Connectivity and permissions

  • The app works offline; network access is only used for standard store distribution and to open external links you choose (e.g., App Store or Google Play).
  • The app does not request access to your contacts, location, microphone, camera, or photos.

Retention and control

  • All progress data stays on your device. You can clear it from within the app (reset progress) or by uninstalling the app.
  • If you reinstall the app or switch devices, your progress does not sync automatically.

Children

The app is intended for learners preparing for radio licence exams and is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
